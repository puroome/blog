​화법이란? 다른 사람의 말을 전달하는 방식


직접화법이란? 다른 사람의 말을 인용부호 " "를 사용하여 그대로 전달
           He says, "I am happy." (그가 한 말이 " " 사이에 들어가 있음)


간접화법이란? 다른 사람의 말을 접속사를 사용하여 전달자의 입장에서 바꾸어 말함
           He says that _____ _____ happy.

​


>>>화법전환_평서문<<<


① 동사변경 : say→say / say to→tell
② "피전달문"→that 절
③ 인칭일치 : 피전달문의 1인칭(I, my, me)→전달동사의 주어와 일치
③ 인칭일치 : 피전달문의 2인칭(you, your)→수화자(say to+사람)에 일치
④ 시제일치
⑤ 부사 변경(아래쪽 참고)

​

Tom says, "I'll be back later."

→ Tom says that he'll be back later.


They say, "We are against his suggestion."

→ They say that they are against his suggestion.


He said, "The meal is ready."
→ He said that the meal was ready.

​
He said to me, "I have seen you."
→ He told me that he had seen me.


Dick said to her, "I can drive now."

→ Dick told her that he could drive then.

​
She said, "I will tell him exactly what I think."
→ She said that she would tell him exactly what she thought.

​
Bill said, "She went to New York to attend the annual meeting."
→ Bill said that she had gone to New York to attend the annual meeting.


They said to him, "We haven't finished our breakfast yet."

→ They told him that they hadn't finished their breakfast yet.


>>>화법전환_의문문<<<


① 동사변경 : say to→ask
②-① 의문사(when, which, what, where, who) 있는 의문문
    → 의문사+주어+동사 순서로 변경(간접의문문 명사절)
②-② 의문사 없는 의문문
    → if(whether) + 주어 + 동사(명사절)
②-③ 부탁/권유 의문문
    → ask + 목적어 + to do


He asked me, "Is your son in?"

​→ He asked me if my son was in.

​
He said to me, "Are you fond of this music?"
→ He asked me if(whether) I was fond of that music.

​

He asked me, "Have you seen her?" and I said, "No."

→ He asked me if I had seen her and I said that I hadn't.

​

​He said to me, "Who is he?"

→ He asked me who he was.

​
He says to me, "What do you want to eat for dinner?"
→ He asks me what I want to eat for dinner.

​
She said to me, "How have you been doing lately?"
→ She asked him how he had been doing lately.

​
"What shall I do?" she asked me.
→ She asked me what she should do.

 = She asked me what to do.


>>>화법전환_명령문<<<

​

충고 -> advise

부탁, 요청 -> ask

명령 -> order

지시 -> tell


ⓐ 일반적 명령문 → tell + 목적어 + to do

​He said to us, "Sit down."

→ He told us to sit down.

​
ⓑ 상관이 부하에게 → order(command) + 목적어 + to do

​She said to them, "Don't smoke."

→ She ordered them not to smoke.

​
ⓒ 의사가 환자에게 충고 → advice + 목적어 + to do

​The doctor said, "You must stay in bed for a few days, boys."

​→ The doctor adviced the boys to stay in bed for a few days.

​
ⓓ 제안, 제의(Let's) → suggest + that + we + should + V 또는 suggest + v-ing

​Tom said, "Let's go out."

→ Tom suggested that we should go out.

 = Tom suggested going out.
