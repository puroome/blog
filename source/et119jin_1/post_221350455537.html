He is the man (who, whom) I thought was honest.

​

이런 문제의 경우

관계사 뒤에

주어가 나와 있으니

whom을 동그라미 치는 경우가 있어요.

He is the man who I thought was honest.

= He is a man. + (I thought) he was honest.

He is the man who(m) I thought honest.

= He is a man. + I thought him honest.

​

▷ 형태가 '관대 + S + V + V'가 아니에요.

▷ 즉, 관계사가 thought의 목적어

​

구분이 어려우신 분들은

이렇게 생각해보세요.

​

She is the author (who/whom) I believe is most likely to receive the award.

​

관계대명사의 답은 who

​

She is the author.

+ I believe (that) "she" is most likely to receive the award.

→ She is the author "who" I believe is most likely to receive the award.

​

만약 목적격이라면

She is the author.

+ I believe "her" to be most likely to receive the award.

→ She is the author "whom" I believe to be most likely to receive the award.
