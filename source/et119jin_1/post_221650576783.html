​

He had two sons, both of whom were killed in the war.

= He had two sons, and both of them were killed in the war.

​

​

A large crowd of people gathered in the park, most of whom were wearing red T-shirts.

= A large crowd of people gathered in the park, and most of them were wearing red T-shirts.

많은 사람들이 공원에 모였는데 그들 대부분은 빨간 셔츠를 입고 있었다.

​

He opened all the boxes, none of which contained the picture he had been looking for.

= He opened all the boxes, but none of them contained the picture he had been looking for.

그는 상자를 모두 열어 봤으나 그 중 어느 곳에도 찾고 있던 사진이 없었다.

​
