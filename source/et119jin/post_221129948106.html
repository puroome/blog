​

burglar의 영영사전 ≫≫ someone who enters a building illegally in order to steal things

housebreaker의 영영사전 ≫≫ a thief who gets into people’s houses by breaking windows, door locks, etc.

​

영영사전 ≫≫ someone who takes money or property illegally, often by using threats or violence

강도가 갑자기 그에게 덤벼들어 돈을 훔쳐갔다.

■ pickpocket 소매치기

영영사전 ≫≫ someone who steals money and other things from people’s pockets and bags, especially in crowded places
