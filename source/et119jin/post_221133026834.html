오늘은

not until 도치에 대해

알아볼게요.

​

혹시 도치 내용이 필요하시면

대부분의

부정어구 도치는

앞에서 일어나죠.

He is never happy.

→ Never is he happy.

He never saw it.

→ Never did he see it.

​

I can hardly understand it.

→ Hardly can I understand it.

​

He has never traveled to Europe.

→ Never has he traveled to Europe.

그럼

not until도 그럴까요?

​

우선

Not until의 형태를 살펴볼게요.

​

첫번째 형태의 예문을 살펴보면

​

They did not know anything about it until yesterday.

→ Not until yesterday did they know anything about it.

​

He did not hear the shocking news until this morning.

→ Not until this morning did he hear the shocking news.

​

주의할 점은

not until + 주어1 + 동사1 + ... + 동사2 + 주어2 + ...인 경우

도치는 뒤(동사2 + 주어2)에서 일어나요.

​

She did not learn to skate until she was ten.

→ Not until she was ten did she learn to skate.

→ Not until was she ten she learned to skate. (X)

​

꼭 기억하셔야 해요!!

​

그럼 중간에 Quiz 하나 풀어볼게요. 틀린 부분을 고쳐보세요.

1. Not until he arrived at home realized he that he forgot to bring his suitcase.

2. Not until did she get a call from John did she send him the document.

​

쉽게 푸셨나요?

​

다시 얘기하지만

Not until에서 주어, 동사의 형태가 2개가 있으면 도치는 뒤에서 일어납니다.

Not until + 주어1 + 동사1 + …… + 동사2 + 주어2

​

따라서 위 문제의 정답은

1. Not until he arrived at home realized he that he forgot to bring his suitcase.

▷ realized he → did he realize

= He did not realized that he forgot to bring his suitcase until he arrived at home.

​

2. Not until did she get a call from John did she send him the document.

▷ 주어, 동사 형태가 2개 있으므로 도치는 앞이 아닌 뒤에요. did she get → she got
