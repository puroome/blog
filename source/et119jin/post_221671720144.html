1. 매달 집세를 내야 하기 때문에, 그는 편의점에서 파트타이머로 일한다. (pay, rent, month)

Because he has to pay the rent every month, he works at a convenient store as a part-time worker.

≫≫ Having to pay the rent every month, he works at a convenient store as a part-time worker.

​

2. 비록 넗지는 않지만, 그 방은 편안해 보인다. (spacious, comfortable)

Although it was not spacious, the room looks comfortable.

≫≫ Not being spacious, the room looks comfortable.

​

3. 플라스틱으로 만들어졌지만, 이 제품은 강철만큼 튼튼하다. (make, strong)

Although it is made from plastic, this product is as strong as steel.

≫≫ (Being) Made from plastic, this product is as strong as steel.

4. 비록 어제 매우 추웠지만, 우리는 공원에서 축구시합을 하느라 재미있었다. (cold, fun)

≫≫ It being very cold, we had fun playing soccer at the park.
