Movie Invitation


Mason: What are you doing tomorrow night?


Emily: Nothing much. Why do you ask?


Mason: How about going to the movies?


Emily: Which movie?


Mason: I think ''Now You See Me 2'' would be a good choice.


Emily: That sounds pretty good. I've seen the trailer.


Mason: Where should we meet?


Emily: I can pick you up. Is 6:00 p.m. Is that alright for you?


Mason: Perfect!
