쇼핑몰 센터 자주 사용되는 영어단어

​

Mall (몰):

Let's meet at the mall entrance.

(몰 입구에서 만나요.)

​

Store (가게):

I'm going to the clothing store to buy a new shirt.

(새 셔츠를 사러 의류 가게에 갈 거예요.)

​

Department store (백화점):

The department store has a wide variety of products.

(백화점은 다양한 제품을 판매합니다.)

​

Shopping center (쇼핑 센터):

The new shopping center has opened downtown.

(새로운 쇼핑 센터가 도심에 개장했어요.)

​

Retailer (소매상):

There are many retailers in the mall selling electronics.

(몰에는 전자제품을 판매하는 많은 소매상이 있어요.)

​

Customer (고객):

The customer is looking for assistance in the shoe section.

(고객이 구두 코너에서 도움을 요청하고 있어요.)

​

Sales (할인):

The store is having a big sales event this weekend.

(이번 주말에는 큰 할인 행사가 있어요.)

​

Promotion (프로모션):

They're offering a special promotion for loyalty cardholders.

(충성 카드 소지자를 위한 특별 프로모션을 진행하고 있어요.)

​

Checkout (계산대):

Let's go to the checkout counter to pay for our items.

(물건 값을 내기 위해 계산대로 가요.)

​

Cashier (계산원):

Please take your receipt from the cashier.

(계산원에게 영수증을 받아가세요.)

​

Shopping cart (장바구니):

Don't forget to grab a shopping cart for your groceries.

(식료품을 담을 장바구니를 가져가는 걸 잊지 마세요.)

​

Browse (둘러보다):

I like to browse different stores before making a purchase.

(구매 전에 다양한 가게를 둘러보는 걸 좋아해요.)

​

Restroom (화장실):

Excuse me, where is the restroom?

(실례지만, 화장실이 어디에 있나요?)

​

Sale associate (판매원):

The sales associate can help you find the right size.

(판매원이 적절한 사이즈를 찾아줄 수 있어요.)

(어디에서 ...을 찾을 수 있을까요?)

​

(이걸 반품/교환하려고요)

​

Can I pay with a credit card/cash?

(신용카드/현금으로 결제할 수 있을까요?)

​

It's a bit pricey.

(조금 비싸요)

(재고를 확인해볼게요)

(이걸로 할게요)

(영수증 좀 받을 수 있을까요?)

That's a good deal.

쇼핑몰 센터 상황별 다이얼로그

상황 1: 물품을 구매하려는 상황

Customer (고객): Excuse me, I'm looking for a new laptop. Can you recommend a reliable brand?

​

Salesperson (판매원): Certainly! We have a wide range of laptops from various brands. Are you looking for something specific in terms of specifications or budget?

​

Customer (고객): I need a laptop for everyday use, and my budget is around $1000.

​

Salesperson (판매원): In that case, I would recommend checking out our selection of laptops from XYZ brand. They offer good performance and fall within your budget.

상황 2: 할인 이벤트에 대한 문의

Customer (고객): Hi, I heard there's a sale going on. Can you tell me more about it?

​

Store Assistant (매장 직원): Yes, we're currently running a summer sale where you can get up to 50% off on selected items. Is there anything specific you're interested in?

​

Customer (고객): I'm looking for a new pair of sneakers. Are there any discounts on footwear?

​

Store Assistant (매장 직원): Absolutely! We have a wide range of discounted sneakers in various styles and brands. Feel free to browse and find the perfect pair for you.

상황 3: 환불 및 교환 요청

Customer (고객): Hi, I bought this shirt yesterday, but it doesn't fit me well. Can I exchange it for a different size?

​

Cashier (계산원): I'm sorry to hear that. Do you have the receipt and tags for the shirt?

​

Customer (고객): Yes, I have the receipt and the tags are still attached.

​

Cashier (계산원): Perfect. You can go to the customer service desk, and they will assist you with the exchange process and help you find the right size.

상황 4: 계산대에서 결제

Cashier (계산원): That will be $45.99. How would you like to pay?

​

Customer (고객): Can I pay with a credit card?

​

Cashier (계산원): Of course. Please insert your card into the chip reader and enter your PIN number when prompted.

​

Customer (고객): Here's my card. Thank you.

​

Cashier (계산원): Thank you. Your payment was successful. Here's your receipt. Have a great day!
