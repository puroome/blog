자주 사용되는 영어 회화 표현:

​

Can I have two tickets for the 7 PM show, please?

(7시 상영회의 티켓 두 장을 주문할 수 있을까요?)

​

Where is the box office located?

(매표소는 어디에 있나요?)

​

Are there any seats available for the next show?

​

(다음 상영회에 좌석이 있나요?)

Could I get a large popcorn and two sodas, please?

(팝콘 한 컵과 탄산음료 두 개를 주문할 수 있을까요?)

​

The movie starts in five minutes. Hurry up!

(영화가 5분 후에 시작합니다. 서둘러요!)

​

Excuse me, could you please turn off your phone? It's distracting.

(실례합니다, 핸드폰을 끄시겠어요? 집중하기 어려워요.)

​

Enjoy the show!

(공연을 즐기세요!)

​

(영화 정말 멋있었어요. 영화가 끝날 때까지 계속 긴장되었어요.)

​

The special effects in that film were mind-blowing.

(그 영화의 특수 효과는 놀라웠어요.)

​

The theater is usually crowded on weekends, so it's best to arrive early.

(극장은 주말에 보통 붐빕니다, 그래서 일찍 도착하는 게 좋아요.)

티켓 구매

A: Excuse me, I'd like to buy two tickets for tonight's show.

B: Sure, which section would you like? We have balcony and orchestra seats available.

A: I'll take two balcony seats, please.

B: Alright, that will be $40. Enjoy the show!

​

좌석 안내

A: Excuse me, I'm looking for my seat. Can you help me?

B: Of course! What's your seat number?

A: It's B12.

B: Alright, your seat is on the right side, row B, seat number 12. Enjoy the performance!

​

인터미션에서 대화

A: That was an amazing first act, don't you think?

B: Absolutely! The actors were brilliant, and the set design was stunning.

A: I couldn't agree more. Do you want to grab some snacks during the intermission?

B: Sounds good. Let's meet at the concession stand in ten minutes.

​

공연 중 예기치 않은 사건

A: (중간에 소리가 나면서 연기 중단)

B: What's happening? Why did the performance stop?

A: I think there's a technical issue with the stage lighting. They're working on fixing it.

B: I hope they resolve it soon. I was really enjoying the show.

​

공연 종료 후 인사

A: (배우들이 다시 나와 인사)

B: Bravo! That was an outstanding performance!

A: Yes, they truly gave it their all. I'm glad we came to see this show.

B: Me too. Let's give them a standing ovation to show our appreciation.

​

공연 후 소감 나누기

A: How did you like the play?

B: It was phenomenal! The acting was top-notch, and the storyline was captivating.

A: I completely agree. I was on the edge of my seat the entire time.

B: We should definitely come back for more shows in the future.
