Fulfilling Tasks 관련 영어회화 및 유용한 표현





1. Fulfilling General Tasks


Carter: Excuse me sir. I already finished the report you asked me to do.


Mr. Bell: Oh that’s great. Please email it to me and give me a hard copy as well.


Carter: Yes, sir. Right away. But first can you please sign these papers?


Mr. Bell: Sure.



2. Holding a Meeting


Mr. Bell: Let me get to the point. We are here to welcome our new employee,


Carter. He will be working with us starting today.


Carter: Thank you. Nice to meet you everyone. 

I look forward to working with you.

 I hope we will have a great time working together.




Fulfilling Tasks 관련  유용한 표현

 


The following are sample phrases and sentences related to fulfilling tasks

at work and holding meetings. Practice saying each. Think of scenarios

where you can use them.


• Please finish off the task by Friday.


• Please have it done before the deadline.


• I’m going on a business trip.


• Can you please check this again?


• Do you mind if I get another copy?


• You need to sign these papers.


• Let me know if you need any help.


• Could you organize the applicants’ résumés?


• Could you give me a copy of the minutes of the meeting?


• We have a meeting later, 3:00 pm.


• Leave it to me.


• I can do it.


• I’ll finish it no matter what.


• I’m done with the reports.

• Do you want me to email you the files I created?


• We have a weekly meeting every Friday.


• Mr. ___ , can we get your input on this topic?


• Do you have something you would like to add?


• Thank you for your participation.
