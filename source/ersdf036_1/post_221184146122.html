1. Appointment Agreement


Nolan: When are you free? I need to talk to you about something.


Brad: Let’s meet later at 5:00 pm. I have to finish some tasks.


Nolan: Alright. See you later.


2. Postponing/Cancelling an Appointment


Brad: Hello? I’m sorry. 

Can we move our appointment to tomorrow? 

I’m notyet finished with some work. Or  if you want, you can wait for me until 6:30.


Nolan: I can wait for you. I have nothing to do anyway.


3. Being Late at an Appointment


Brad: Hi. I’m sorry it took me so long.


Nolan: It’s fine. Let’s eat first. I’m hungry.

The following are sample phrases and sentences used when scheduling

an appointment, whether it is formal or informal.


• Do you have time?


• I’ll see you at…


• Let’s meet at…


• Do you have an appointment tomorrow?


• I’m sorry but could we cancel our appointment today?


• Sorry I’m late.


• Pardon. I have a full schedule this week.


• Sorry but I don’t have extra time this weekend. I already have plans.


• Can we meet later?


• Hello. Sorry I’m late.


• When can I see you?


• What time is good for you?


• What time would you like me to drop by?


• Sorry but I can’t today.


• Let me remind you that we have an appointment today.


• I will be late today. I’m sorry.


• How soon can you make it?


• I’ll arrange my schedule to fit yours.


• Have you been waiting long?


• Let’s meet again some time.
