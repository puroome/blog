Erin: I’m not sure. When I’m in the car,

 it sounds like it’s coming from the engine,

but when I get out of the car,

 it sounds like it’s coming from directly under the trunk maybe the muffler?


Bob: That’s not a noise a muffler would make. 

Let me take a look. Can you start the

Erin: Sure.

• I want to have my car checked.

• My battery is dead.


• How much is the inspection fee?


• I think the oil is leaking.

• Can you check if there’s something wrong with my car?

• My car keeps on making weird noises.
