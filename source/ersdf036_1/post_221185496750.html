Financial Issues 금융문제 관련 영어회화 및 유용한표현




 



1. Borrowing Money


Lucas: Could you lend me some money? I’m broke and I need to pay the rent.


Caleb: How much do you need?


Lucas: I need at least $ 650.


Caleb: When will you be able to pay it?


Lucas: Maybe next month, once I’m through with paying my health card.


Caleb: Well, I trust you enough. But please return the money at that date okay?


Lucas: Yes, I will. Thank you very much.



2. Paying

Caleb: Hello. When are you paying me back? It has already been 2 months.


Lucas: I’m sorry. I’ll pay you tomorrow.


Caleb: Please send it immediately. I need the money
