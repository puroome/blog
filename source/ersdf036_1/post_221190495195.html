Vacation/Leave 방학,휴가 영어회화 유용한표현



1. Going on a Vacation/Leave


Layla: I’m thinking of going on a 5-day vacation.


Elle: That’s nice. You finally decided on taking a break from work.


Layla: Yes. I think I need it to refresh myself.


Elle: Have you already filed for vacation leave?


Layla: Yes I have. I hope it would be granted as soon as possible.


2. Filing for Resignation


Max: I’m quitting this job.


Wyatt: You need to file a resignation letter stating your reason for leaving. 


Max: Yes I will.
