Traffic Violation 교통 위반 영어회화 및 유용한표현





Isaac: Is there a problem, officer?


Officer Brock: I stopped you for driving through a red light.


Isaac: Did I really? I had no idea that I ran it.


Officer Brock: Don't you know that yellow means slow down?


Isaac: Yes, sir.


Officer Brock: Why'd you speed up instead?


Isaac: I really have no excuse, sir.


Officer Brock: I'll have to write you a ticket.


Isaac: I understand, and I am very sorry.


Officer Brock: Here's your ticket.





Traffic Violation 교통 위반  유용한표현



The following are useful phrases when talking about traffic rules and

regulations and violations. Think of scenarios where you can use them.

Give other sample phrases as well.



• Please bring out your license and registration.


• You disobeyed a traffic signal.


• What seems to be the problem?


• Do you know why I pulled you over?


• Don’t you know the speed limit in this city?


• Your license is expired.


• I’d like to reclaim my car that was towed.


• How much fine should I pay?


• You are under arrest.


• You are over speeding.


• You have to go to court.


• Sorry. I’m very late for an important meeting.


• I’ll leave you off with a warning.


• I’ll give you a ticket.


• Sorry but I don’t have any identification right now.


• Sorry. I didn’t notice the stop sign.


• Our law strictly doesn’t allow driving under the influence of drugs or alcohol.
