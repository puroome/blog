emergency. Practice saying each expression. Think of more examples.

• Watch out! / Look out!


• She’s not breathing.


• Please help me.


• I need a doctor.


• I’ve cut myself.


• I was robbed.

• I got catnapped.


• I’ve been mugged.


• They’ve been attacked.


• I can do a CPR.


• There’s been an accident.


• Please hurry!


• Is everyone okay?


• Call the police!


• My money has been stolen.


• The building’s on fire.


• Is there a doctor or nurse around here?


• Apply pressure to stop the bleeding.
