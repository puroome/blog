Everyday Life 일상생활 영어회화 및 유용한 표현





Julia: What's the weather like?


Evan: I don't know. I just woke up.


Julia: Why don't you look outside?


Evan: Okay. It looks like rain.


Julia: Why do you say that?


Evan: The sky is gray.


Julia: Is it raining right now?


Evan: No.


Julia: How do you know?


Evan: The street isn't wet.


Julia: I have to go shopping today.


Evan: You'd better take an umbrella.
