Gabriel: Kids! it’s time to eat your breakfast! We’re going out!

Oliver: Where are we going?

Ysa: Where do you want to go? We could go on to an amusement park or a

waterpark. Which one do you want?

Kylie: Oh my! I want to go to a waterpark!

Oliver: Me too!

Gabriel: Alright! Pack your things and we will leave at 8:00.

Ysa: But you have to finish your food first.

The following are sample phrases you can use when talking about family

life. Practice saying each. Can you add more examples on the list?



• Time to wake up!


• Please turn off the lights.


• We’re going to…


• Where’s the remote?


• Have you done the laundry?


• I will give you a present once you finish cleaning your room.


• Please turn down the sound.


• Could you answer the phone?


• Where are you?


• Wash your hands.


• Scrub all over your body.


• Could you get me a towel?


• I’m brushing my teeth.


• We’re out of soap.

• I need to go to the grocery store to buy the things we need.


• It’s way past your bedtime.


• You have to go early to school.


• Separate your laundry before washing them.


• Let’s do a general cleaning.


• Take out the garbage.


• Turn the radio off
