1. Undergoing an Interview


Carter: Good morning, I am Carter and I’m here for my interview.


Evan: Hello, nice to meet you.

 I’m Evan. So why don't you tell me why you are

interested in changing positions?


Carter: Unfortunately, our company is shutting down due to the economy.


Evan: What would you consider your strengths?


Carter: I am probably best at researching for marketing purposes.


Evan: What is your biggest weakness?


Carter: I tend to get bored easily and so love to keep myself challenged.


Evan: We have a position where that could work out well.


2. Asking Inquiries


Carter: How much is the salary?


Evan: It depends on your performance. But it’s a sure $1,000 a month after taxes.


Carter: What are the perks that you offer?


Evan: We provide full health insurance - medical, dental, and disability.


Carter: Thank you for this opportunity.
