Undergoing Physical Check-up 건강진담 병원에서 유용한 영어회화 및 표현



 




Dr. Hall: Good morning, how are you doing today?


Ciara: I have been feeling pretty good, Dr. Hall.


Dr. Hall: So, I can see by your chart that you are here for your annual physical.


Ciara: Yes, I am playing on the tennis team this year,

 and they are requiring me to get a physical exam.


Dr. Hall: We are basically going to check your heart, lungs, blood sugar levels, and


eyes, ears, and nose.


Ciara: I've kind of been having problems being out of breath. Can you look at that?


Dr. Hall: Yes, I will check you for asthma, and maybe we can check you for allergies.


Ciara: Thank you. What will the blood test tell you?


Dr. Hall: We will determine blood sugar, cholesterol, and white blood cell count.
