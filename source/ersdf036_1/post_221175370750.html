Health Club 헬스장 영어회화 및 유용한표현







Don: Hello, welcome to the gym.


Sean: Hello. I am interested in signing up for a membership.


Don: Great! Do you want sign up for this branch?


Sean: Yes. I also work in the city so I want to know 

if I can also work out there with a

single membership?


Don: Yes you can. You can work out at any of our gyms in the city.


Sean: That's wonderful! How much is the charge?


Don: The fee is $100 per month, but if you sign up for 6 months we give you a 20% discount.


Sean: That sounds good. I will sign up for six months.




Health Club 헬스장  유용한표현







The following are useful expressions you can use at a gym, fitness center

or a health club. Practice saying each statement and think of more

examples that you can add.


• Can you tell me about…


• I'd like some information about…


• Do you have any special introductory packages?


• What sort of membership options do you have?


• How often can I use the gym with this membership?


• How much does a weekly / monthly membership cost?


• How much would it cost to pay each time I use the gym?


• I’d like to lose weight / get fit / tone up / build stamina / increase muscle

strength

What do you suggest for… (weight loss / weight training)?


• What sort of facilities do you have?


• Can you show me how to use the equipment?


• How late do you stay open?


• Are you open at the weekend as well?


• Do you run any classes?
