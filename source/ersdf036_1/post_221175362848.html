Paul: What’ll it be today, ma’am?


Reese: Ten dollars worth of unleaded, please. Here’s the key to the tank.


Paul: Coming right up… Check the oil for you today?


Reese: Yes, please, and could you get the windshield, too?


Paul: Sure thing. Open the hood for me, will you? There’s a lever inside there.


Reese: Oh, yes, of course.


Paul: Okay, ma’am, that’ll be ten dollars.


Reese: Here you are.


Paul: Thanks. You take care now.
