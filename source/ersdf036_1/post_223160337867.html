​

​

​

등산할때 상황별 다이얼로그

​

상황: 등산로에서 길을 잃은 상황

A: Hey, I think we might have taken a wrong turn. The trail markers disappeared.

B: Oh no, let's consult the map. We need to find our way back on track.

A: It looks like we need to backtrack a bit and take the left fork instead of the right one.

B: Good catch! Let's be more careful and pay attention to the trail markers from now on.

​

상황: 등산 중 예상치 못한 날씨 변화

A: The weather is changing rapidly. It looks like a storm is approaching.

B: We should find a sheltered area as soon as possible. Is there a nearby cave or a designated shelter?

A: I'm not sure. Let me check the map. It says there's a small cave about half a mile ahead.

B: Let's pick up the pace and make it there before the rain starts pouring.

​

상황: 등산 중 다른 등산객과의 만남

A: Excuse me, do you know how much farther it is to the summit?

B: We're almost there. Just a steep section ahead, but the view is totally worth it!

A: That's great to hear. How long did it take you to reach this point?

B: It took us about two hours. Take your time and enjoy the hike. You'll love the view at the top.

​

상황: 등산 중 다친 동료

A: Oh no, John just tripped and hurt his ankle. We need to help him.

B: Let's take out the first aid kit and assess the injury. Is it swollen or bleeding?

A: It looks swollen, but I don't see any bleeding. We should immobilize his ankle and call for help.

B: I'll call emergency services while we wrap his ankle with the compression bandage from the kit. Hang in there, John. Help is on the way.

​

등산 중 발생할 수 있는 다양한 상황에 대비하여 대화를 준비하는 것은 중요합니다. 실제 상황에서는 안전을 최우선으로 생각하고 전문가의 도움을 요청하는 것이 가장 좋습니다.
