상황별생활영어회화- at school






학교생활에서 있을법한 회화표현을 배워보아요.
 
1. Enrollment

Chang: I’d like to enroll in this school.
(저는 이학교 입학하고 싶습니다)

Mr. Nolan: Here’s a pamphlet showing our admission guidelines.
(여기 우리의 입학 지침서를 보여 주는 팜플렛이 있어요.)

Chang: When is your admission deadline?
(입학 마감이 언제까지죠?)

Mr. Nolan: The last enrollment day is on July 22nd
(마지막 등록날짜는 7월22일이에요)

배워두면 좋은표현:
admission deadline (입학 마감일)
admission guidelines (입학 지침서,입학 안내문)



2. Attending School

Ms. Emily: Good morning everyone. Before I walk you through the class rules,
I want you to introduce yourselves first.
(안녕하세요.여러분,제가 학급 규칙을 설명하기전에,각자 자기소개를 먼저 해주세요.)

Chang: Let me start. Hello. My name is Chang Il Seok.
(제가 먼저할게요. 제이름은 장일석이에요.)


3. Taking an Exam

Mae: How did you prepare for the exam?
(너 시작준비 어떻게 했니?)

Chang: I stayed up all night to study. Now I think I’m ready.
(나는 공부할려고 밤샜어,난 지금준비된것 같아.)


4. Getting Grades

Chang: Wow. Hard work really pays off. Now I got high grades!
( 와우 열심히 하니가 되네, 나 높은점수 받았어.)
Ben: Congratulations! You deserve it.
(축하해)
