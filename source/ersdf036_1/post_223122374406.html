​

[장면: 친구들이 함께하는 카페]

​

A: Hey, I heard about your recent job interview. How did it go?

B: It didn't go as well as I had hoped. I'm feeling really disappointed.

A: I understand how you feel, but don't be too hard on yourself.

Remember, every interview is a learning experience. You'll do better next time.

​

B: Thanks, I really needed to hear that. It's just frustrating to face rejection.

A: I know it can be tough, but remember that even successful people have faced rejection.

It's not a reflection of your abilities. Keep pushing forward, and you'll find the right opportunity.

​

A: I heard about your recent breakup. I'm so sorry to hear that.

B: Yeah, it's been really tough. I feel heartbroken and lost.

A: Breakups are never easy, but remember that time heals wounds.

Focus on self-care and surround yourself with friends who love and support you. You'll find happiness again.

​

B: I appreciate your support. It's hard to imagine moving on right now.

A: I completely understand. Take all the time you need to heal, but also remember that there are brighter days ahead. You're a strong person, and you'll find love and happiness again when the time is right.

​

A: I noticed you've been feeling stressed about your workload lately.

B: Yeah, it's been overwhelming. I feel like I'm constantly juggling tasks.

A: I want you to know that I believe in you. You're incredibly capable and have always managed to overcome challenges. Take it one step at a time, and remember to prioritize self-care. You'll get through this.

​

B: Thank you for your kind words. Sometimes it's hard to see the light at the end of the tunnel.

A: I know it may seem tough right now, but remember that you've faced difficult times before and emerged stronger. Keep your focus and stay positive. You have the resilience to overcome any obstacles that come your way.

​

A: 안녕, 최근 면접에 대해 들었어. 어떻게 되었어?

B: 내가 기대한 대로 잘 안 됐어. 실망스러워서 정말 기분이 좋지 않아.

A: 네 기분을 이해하지만, 자책하지 마세요. 기억해, 모든 면접은 배우는 경험이에요.

다음에는 더 잘 할 수 있을 거예요.

​

B: 고마워, 이런 말을 들어야 했어. 거절을 마주하면 답답하긴 하지만,

성공한 사람들도 거절을 경험해왔어요. 그건 당신의 능력을 반영하는 것이 아니에요.

앞으로 나아가며 찾게 될 적절한 기회를 찾을 거예요.

​

A: 최근 이별에 대해 들었어. 정말 안타깝네.

B: 응, 정말 힘들어. 가슴이 아파서 어둡고 허전해.

A: 이별은 언제나 쉽지 않지만, 기억해, 시간이 상처를 치유해줄 거예요.

스스로를 돌봐주고, 사랑하고 지지해주는 친구들을 둘러싸고 계속 나아가세요. 다시 행복을 찾을 거예요.

​

B: 당신의 지원에 감사해요. 지금은 움직일 수 없는 상황이라고 상상하기 어려워요.

A: 완전히 이해해요. 치유할 시간이 필요하지만,

또한 미래에 더 밝은 날들이 기다리고 있음을 기억하세요. 당신은 강인한 사람이고,

적절한 시기에 다시 사랑과 행복을 찾을 수 있을 거예요.

​

A: 최근에 업무 부담으로 인해 스트레스를 느낀 것 같아서 알았어.

B: 응, 너무 압박감에 휩싸여서 마치 일을 계속 던져주는 것 같아.

A: 내가 너를 믿는다는 걸 알아줬으면 좋겠어. 당신은 믿을 수 없을 만큼 능력이 있으며, 항상 도전을 극복해왔어.
