은행 이용시 필수적 알아야 하는 회화표현

예금 (Deposits):

I'd like to make a deposit into my savings account, please.

(저축 계좌에 예금을 하려고 합니다.)

​

How much would you like to deposit?

(얼마를 예금하시겠습니까?)

​

Can I get a receipt for my deposit?

(예금에 대한 영수증을 받을 수 있을까요?)

​

인출 (Withdrawals):

I'd like to withdraw $100 from my checking account.

(당좌 계좌에서 100달러를 인출하고 싶습니다.)

​

Could you give me a mix of $10 and $20 bills, please?

(10달러와 20달러 지폐를 혼합해서 주실 수 있을까요?)

​

I'll need to enter my PIN for the withdrawal.

(인출을 위해 PIN 번호를 입력해야 합니다.)

​

대출 (Loans):

I'm interested in applying for a personal loan.

(개인 대출 신청에 관심이 있습니다.)

​

What are the interest rates for a car loan?

(차량 대출의 이자율은 어떻게 되나요?)

​

How long is the repayment period for this loan?

(이 대출의 상환 기간은 어떻게 되나요?)

​

환전 (Currency Exchange):

I'd like to exchange US dollars for euros.

(미국 달러를 유로로 환전하고 싶습니다.)

​

What is the current exchange rate for pounds to dollars?

(파운드에서 달러로의 현재 환율은 어떻게 되나요?)

​

Could you please give me a breakdown of the exchange fees?

(환전 수수료에 대한 내역을 알려주시겠어요?)

​

기타 (Other):

Can I get a bank statement for the past three months?

(지난 세 달간의 은행 명세서를 받을 수 있을까요?)

​

Is there an ATM nearby where I can withdraw cash?

(현금을 인출할 수 있는 근처 ATM이 있나요?)

​

I'd like to open a bank account, please.

​

Could you help me with a transaction?

(거래에 도움을 받을 수 있을까요?)

​

How much is the minimum balance required?

(최소 잔액은 얼마나 필요한가요?)

​

I'd like to deposit this check into my account.

(이 수표를 계좌에 입금하고 싶습니다.)

​

Can I withdraw $200 from my account?

(계좌에서 200달러를 인출할 수 있을까요?)

​

What's the current exchange rate for dollars to euros?

(달러에서 유로로의 현재 환율은 어떻게 되나요?)

​

I need to transfer money to another account.

(다른 계좌로 돈을 이체해야 합니다.)

​

I'd like to apply for a loan.

​

How much interest do I earn on my savings account?

(저축 계좌에서 얼마나 이자를 받을 수 있나요?)

​

(월별 청구서에 대한 자동이체를 설정하고 싶습니다.)

Can I get some small bills for this large denomination?

(연락처 정보를 업데이트해야 합니다.)

​

​

​

In the red: 적자를 내다.

​

Money talks: 돈이 중요하다는 의미.

​

Put your money where your mouth is: 말에 따라 돈을 내다.

​

Rainy day fund: 비상 자금.

​

Save for a rainy day: 나쁜 일을 대비하여 돈을 모으다.

​

​

Cost an arm and a leg: 매우 비싸다.

​

Make ends meet: 수입과 지출을 균형있게 맞추다.

​

Pay through the nose: 비싸게 지불하다.

​

Rob Peter to pay Paul: 한 쪽에서 돈을 빼서 다른 쪽에 쓰다.

​

Sock away money: 돈을 모아두다.

은행 이용시 상황별 다이얼로그

예금(Deposit):

Customer: Good morning. I'd like to make a deposit into my savings account.

Teller: Good morning. Sure, how much would you like to deposit?

Customer: I'd like to deposit $500.

Teller: Alright. Please fill out this deposit slip and I'll process it for you.

Customer: Thank you.

​

인출(Withdrawal):

Customer: Hi, I'd like to withdraw some cash from my checking account.

Teller: Of course. How much would you like to withdraw?

Customer: I'd like to withdraw $200.

Teller: Alright. Please enter your PIN on the keypad, and I'll get that for you.

Customer: Thank you.

​

대출(Loan):

Customer: Hello. I'm interested in applying for a personal loan.

Bank Officer: Great. We offer competitive interest rates.

Can you tell me how much you need and for what purpose?

Customer: I need $10,000 for home renovations.

Bank Officer: Alright. Let's discuss the terms and repayment options for the loan.

​

환전(Currency Exchange):

Customer: Hi, I'd like to exchange US dollars for euros.

Teller: Sure, I can help you with that. How much would you like to exchange?

Customer: I'd like to exchange $500.

Teller: Alright. Let me check the current exchange rate for you.

​

신용카드(Credit Card):

Customer: Excuse me, I have a question about my credit card bill.

Customer Service: Sure, I'm here to assist you. What seems to be the issue?

Customer: I believe there is an incorrect charge on my statement.

Customer Service: Let me review your account. Please provide me with the details of the transaction.

​

계좌개설(Opening an Account):

Customer: Hi, I'd like to open a new checking account.

Bank Officer: Certainly. We have various options available. What type of checking account are you interested in?

Customer: I'm looking for a basic checking account with no monthly fees.

Bank Officer: I can help you with that. Let's get started with the necessary paperwork.

​

명세서(Statement):

Customer: Good afternoon. I'd like to request a bank statement for the past three months.

Teller: Good afternoon. Sure, I can assist you with that. Could you please provide your account details?

Customer: Here is my account number and identification.

Teller: Thank you. I'll print out the statements for you.

​

온라인 뱅킹(Online Banking):

Customer: Hi, I'd like to set up online banking for my account.

Bank Officer: Absolutely. To get started, you'll need to fill out this online banking registration form.

Customer: Okay. Once I submit it, how long will it take to activate my online banking?

Bank Officer: It usually takes 24 to 48 hours for the activation process.

​

수수료(Fees):

Customer: Hello. I noticed some fees on my account. Could you please explain them to me?

Teller: Of course. Let me review your account. The fees are associated with ATM withdrawals made outside our network.

Customer: I see. Can you provide more details on the fees?

Teller: Certainly. The fee is $3 per transaction made at non-network ATMs.

​

ATM 문제(ATM Issues):

Customer: Excuse me, the ATM just ate my card. What should I do?

Teller: I'm sorry to hear that. Please don't worry. We'll help you resolve this issue. Can you provide me with your card details?

Customer: Here is my card number and identification.

Teller: Thank you. We'll block the card and order a replacement for you.
