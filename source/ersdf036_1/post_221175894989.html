Olivia: Hello. I’d like to take a portrait.


Joseph: Sure. What is it for?


Olivia: It’s for my modeling portfolio.


Joseph: Do you want to take it now?


Olivia: Yes. I’ll just have my make-up retouched a little bit.


Joseph: Alright. Would you like a dark or a white background?


Olivia: A plain white background please.


Joseph: Okay. Let’s set it all up. We have a dressing room at the back if you’d like.


Olivia: Thank you. Give me a moment.







Photographic Studio 사진관 유용한표현


The following are useful phrases and sentences you can use when talking

with a photographer at a photographic studio.


• I’d like to take a formal photo.


• Do you take creative shots?


• Can you take pictures of my baby?


• Do you take graduation photos?


• I’d like to print these photos.


• Do you sell frames?


• Can you print photos in a flash drive?


• I want to have a passport-sized photo.


• What photo sizes do you offer?


• When can I pick them up?


• Do you film weddings?


• What size do you want them in?


• What background design or color would you like to have?


• We offer printing packages.


• We also laminate I.D.s.


• Could you send soft copies by email?


• Hold still.


• Please come in front of the camera.


• Lower your chin a little bit.


• Turn your head to the left a little bit.


• These photos are nice. Thanks.
