Haneul: Good morning. I’d like to have this suit cleaned please. 

It has a coffee stain

on the jacket sleeve, right there.


Mei: Oh yes I see. I’ll make a note of it. 

Here’s your ticket. Be ready on Friday

afternoon.


Haneul: Friday afternoon?!? Why that’s three days from now!


Mei: Because we haven’t a 24 hour service. But I’ll do my best.


Haneul: Okay. Please notify me thru text message when it is finished.







Laundry Shop  세탁소 유용한표현




Here are sample phrases you can use when going to a laundry shop to

have your clothes washed. Practice them and try to think more examples

as well.

• Do you do dry-cleaning?

• How much is it per kilogram?

• How many days do it take for you to

finish the laundry?

• I’d like to have this shirt drycleaned.

• Can you get the stain out?

• I’d like to have these clothes

washed.

• When can I pick them up?

• Can you iron it?

• When can you have it done?

• This cannot be ironed.

• I’d like to have the stains removed.

• Hello. I’m here for my laundry.

• I’ve come for my laundry.

• Here’s your ticket.

• Here’s your laundry.

• Excuse me. The stains weren’t

removed.

• These aren’t my clothes.

• Some other color got washed into

it.

• Thank you for the great service.
