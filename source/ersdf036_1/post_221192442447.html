1. Surgery

Dr. Cooper: Our findings show that you have a perforated ulcer wherein there is a

hole in your stomach due to the growth of ulcer.


Gael: So what should be done doctor?


Dr. Cooper: We need to perform a surgery which involves sewing up the hole.


Gael: Well, that’s pretty terrifying.



2. Neurosurgery


Dr. Lewis: I’m very sorry to say that your son has a benign rolandic epilepsy. We

considered all the symptoms he showed and all the test results.


Adrian: Can it be treated?


Dr. Lewis: Yes. We can perform a neurosurgery.
