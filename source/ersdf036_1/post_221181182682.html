Marriage 결혼 영어회화 및 유용한표현



 




1. Married Life


Aunt Grace: How are the newlyweds?


Beatrice: We’re on cloud nine. We’re glad we finally settled down.


Aunt Grace: I’m so happy for you two. Now you are both married, you have to be


more responsible with your actions. I wish you well.


Beatrice: We will. Thank you.


2. Pregnancy


Liam: Beatrice is pregnant!


Grandpa Simon: Congratulations! You’re going to be a father.


Liam: Thank you! We’re so blessed.
