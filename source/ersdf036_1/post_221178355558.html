Road Accident 교통사고 영어회화 및 유용한표현





Terell: What happened today on the freeway?


Brant: There was a truck on the freeway, and it flipped over.


Terell: Really?

Brant: I wouldn't lie about something like that.


Terell: Were there any injuries?


Brant: I heard that a couple people got hurt.

Terell: Are they all right?


Brant: The news said they should be fine.


Terell: I'm glad to know that everyone made it out okay.

Brant: Me too.
