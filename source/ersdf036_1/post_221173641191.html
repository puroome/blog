1. Opening an Account

Joaquin: Hi. I’d like to open an account.

Teresa: Please fill up this form first. Have your requirements prepared.

2. Depositing Money

Chantal: Hello. I’d like to make a deposit to my savings account.

Rina: Please get a cash deposit slip first and fill it up.

3. Withdrawing Money

Kate: How much can I withdraw at ATMs?

Nate: You may only withdraw up to $1,000 per day.

4. Applying for a Loan

Valerie: Hi. I’d like to apply for a business loan.

Tanya: You have to submit your application along with your requirements.
