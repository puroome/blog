1. Cooking Skills

Kaylee: Do you know how to cook?


Natalie: Yes, I cook well. I can cook a lot of various cuisines.


Kaylee: Oh, good. Please teach me sometimes.



2. Planning What to Cook


Sebastian: What do you want to have today?


Aria: I want an herb-roasted chicken.


Sebastian: Good choice. Alright I’ll prepare it for you.



3. Tasting


Sebastian: How is it?


Aria: Wow! This is so delicious! Plus I love the sauce that you made.


Sebastian: Thank you. It’s a good thing you like it.
