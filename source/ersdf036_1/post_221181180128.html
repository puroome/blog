Dating / Being in  a Relationship 연애 영어회화 및 유용한표현







1. Going on a Date


Liam: I love your style.


Beatrice: Thank you. I didn’t put much effort in it.


Liam: You are so beautiful.


Beatrice: Thank you. You look good too.


2. Expressing Love


Liam: I think of you everyday. I’m so in love with you.


Beatrice: I love you too.


3. Breaking Up


Beatrice: I can’t take it anymore. Let’s break up.


Liam: No please. Think it over. I can’t give you up.


4. Going Strong


Lucy: How are you and Liam doing?


Beatrice: We’re going strong. 

We went through a lot but we love each other too

much to separate.


Lucy: You two are adorable. I’d hold onto that one if I were you.
