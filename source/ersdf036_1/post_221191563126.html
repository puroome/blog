1. Problems with the Stomach


Jules: My stomach hurts.


Dr. Smith: What did you eat?


Jules: I had spaghetti with clam sauce for lunch.


Dr. Smith: It’s possible you have food poisoning. What symptoms do you feel?


Jules: I feel like vomiting, I belch a lot and it hurts all over.




2. Problems with Ears/Nose/Tongue (ENT)


Sid: I have a terrible cold.


Dr. Ruston: Have you taken anything for it?


Sid: Not yet.


Dr. Ruston: Alright. I’ll prescribe you some medicine that you have to drink 3 times a day. 

You have to rest and drink plenty of liquids for a while.
