한국인 자주틀리는 영어회화표현








한국인이 잘못쓰는 간단한 회화표현들을 모아봤습니다~~

=

 한번씩 쭉 읽어보고 잘못써왔던게 있는가 볼까요?




 


1. 여기 어디지?


한국인-Where is here? 


미국인- Where are we?


 


2. 서양인들은 코가 높아. 


한국인-Most Westerners have high noses.


미국인-Most Westerners have long noses.


 


3. 아직 밖에 밝아. 


한국인-  It was still bright outside


미국인- It was still light outside. 


 


4. 보통 미국학생들은 교복을 입지않아. 


한국인- Common students in US don't wear a uniform


미국인- The average students in US don't wear a uniform.


 


5. 잔디 밟지마.  


한국인-Don't step on the grass.


미국인-Keep off the grass.


 


6. 나는 운동에 소질이 없어.


한국인- I have no exercise talent.


미국인- I am not athletic.


 


7. 나한테 너무 기대하지마. 


한국인- Don't expect me too much.


미국인- Don't expect too much from (of ) me.


 


8. 집에 모자를 깜빡 놓고 나왔어. 


한국인-  I forget my hat in the house.


미국인-  I left my hat in the house.


 


9. 한 500달러 정도 빌릴 수 있을까?  


한국인- Lend me some money, for instance 500 dollars.


미국인- Lend me some money, say 500 dollars. 


 


10. 어 나 시간있어. 


한국인- I have a free time.


미국인- I am free.


 


11. (무언가 하려고 했는데) 포기했어의 표현. 


한국인- I wanted to go to Europe last summer, but it was too expensive so I gave up to go.


미국인- I wanted to go to Europe last summer, but it was too expensive so I gave up the idea.


 


12. 오늘 신문에 대만에 대한 기사가 있더라. 


한국인- Today's newspapers has his articles on Taiwan.


미국인- Today's newspapers carries his articles on Taiwan.


 


13. 나 초록색 좋아해. 


한국인- I like green color.


미국인- I like green.


 


14. 너 몸무가 얼마나 나가?


한국인- How heavy are you ?


미국인- How much do you weigh ?


 


15. 하얀선 안에서 기다리세요. 


한국인- Please wait inside the white line.


미국인- Please wait behind the white line.


 


16. 너희 집 화재보험 들어있어?


한국인- Is your house insured for fire ?


미국인- Is your house insured against fire ?


 


17. 오는 길에 교통사고가 있었어. 그게 늦은 이유야.  


한국인- That was a traffic accident on my way here. It is why I was late.


미국인- That was a traffic accident on my way here. That is why I was late.


 


18. 어제밤에 일기를 안썼다. 


한국인- Last night I didn't keep my diary.


미국인- Last night I didn't write anything in my diary.


 


19. 그 기차는 한 시간 정도 늦었다. 


한국인- The train was late about an hour.


미국인- The train was about an hour late.


 


20. 너 왜 그렇게 말해?  


한국인- Why do you say like that.


미국인- Why do you say that.


 


21. 얼굴 더러워. 거울 좀 봐. 


한국인-  What a dirty face ! Look at the mirror.


미국인- What a dirty face ! Look in the mirror.


 


22. 그녀의 결혼은 행복해. 


한국인-  Her marriage was happy.


미국인- Her married life was happy.


 


23. 그 클럽 회원수가 55명이야. 


한국인-  The club's members are 55.


미국인-  There are 55 members in the club.


 


24. 이게 바로 네가 잘못한 부분이야. 


한국인-  This is where you mistake.


미국인- This is where you are mistaken.


 


25. 2시간 정도 더 주세요. 


한국인-  Please allow me more two hours.


미국인- Please allow me two more hours.


 


26. 시계가 안가네. 문제가 몬지 확인해야겠다. 


한국인- My watch doesn't move at all. I must get it checked up.


미국인- My watch isn't running, I must get it checked up.


 


27. 그 사람 우리 둘다(공통) 친구야. 


한국인- He is our common friend.


미국인- He is our mutual friend.
