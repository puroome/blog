Caleb: Good morning.


Jose: Good morning. What can I do for you today?


Caleb: I just need a haircut.


Jose: Sure. How would you like me to cut it?


Caleb: Short, please.


Jose: Yes, Sir. I see your hair’s quite rough. I’ll shampoo it first.


Caleb: No problem. Just make sure you cut it short but not too short.




Hair Salon 미용실 유용한표현



Here are some useful phrases when communicating at a hair salon.

• How would you like your hair cut?

• What can I do for you today?

• Do you want a manicure or a

pedicure?

• Do you want to color your hair

today?

• I want a trim.

• Can you cut about 3 inches off

length?

• I want 1 inch off the top and the

sides blended in.

• Can you layer my hair?

• Use a special care in your hair.

• I want curls.

• I would like a straight perm today.

• Can you color my hair to brown?

• I want to get my nails done.

• I want a pedicure with brown nail

polish please.

• What do you think is the color that

would suit my complexion?

• Are you sure that haircut will suit

me?

• Would you like a shampoo that will

make your dandruff disappear.

• I want a whole new look.
