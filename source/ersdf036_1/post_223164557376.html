미용실에 방문할 때 사용되는 몇 가지 영어 단어와 표현을 알려드리겠습니다.

물론 미용실마다 사용되는 용어가 다를 수 있으므로 일반적으로 많이 사용되는 단어들을 안내해드리겠습니다.

​

Haircut: 머리를 자르는 것, 머리 컷팅

Hairstyle: 헤어스타일

Trim: 손질, 잘라주기 (보통 머리 끝을 살짝 자르는 것을 의미)

Bangs (fringe): 앞머리

Layers: 레이어 (머리의 층을 내는 스타일)

Perm: 영구 파마

Color/Dye: 염색

Highlights: 하이라이트 (머리에 밝은 색을 넣는 것)

Balayage: 발라야쥬 (머리에 자연스럽게 염색하는 기법)

Shampoo: 샴푸

Conditioner: 컨디셔너

Blow-dry: 블로우 드라이 (헤어 드라이어로 머리 말리기)

Styling: 스타일링

Comb: 빗

Brush: 브러시

Razor: 면도기 (일부 스타일링에 사용)

Gel: 젤

Hairspray: 헤어스프레이

Salon: 미용실

Barber: 이발사 (남성 미용사)

이러한 단어들을 참고하여 미용실에서 원하는 스타일을 설명하거나 의사소통하는 데 도움이 될 것입니다.

미용사와 원활한 대화를 나누어 원하는 결과를 얻으시길 바랍니다!

미용실에서 자연스럽고 원활한 대화를 나누기 위해 유용한 영어 회화 표현과 숙어를 알려드리겠습니다:

​

예약하기 (Making an appointment):

Hi, I'd like to make an appointment for a haircut, please.

When is the earliest available slot?

Do you have any openings tomorrow?

​

스타일 상담 (Style consultation):

I'm thinking of getting a new hairstyle. What do you recommend?

I want to go for a more natural look with some layers.

Could you show me some pictures of different haircuts?

​

원하는 스타일 설명하기 (Describing the desired style):

I'd like to keep the length but add some layers and get a side-swept fringe.

I'm interested in getting highlights that blend well with my natural color.

Can you help me achieve a sleek and straight look?

​

헤어 컬러 (Hair color):

I want to dye my hair a shade lighter/darker.

Could you recommend a color that suits my skin tone?

I'm interested in trying balayage for a more natural look.

​

잘라주기 (Getting a haircut):

Please just trim the ends, and don't take off too much length.

I'd like a short bob with some layers.

Can you thin out my hair a bit?

​

세부 요청 (Specific requests):

Could you style my hair with loose curls?

Can you part my hair on the left side?

I prefer a more textured look.

​

마무리 (Finishing touches):

Could you blow-dry my hair, please?

Can I have some gel to hold the style in place?

A light mist of hairspray will do.

​

만족도 표현 (Expressing satisfaction):

I love the way my hair turned out. Thank you!

You did an amazing job. I'm very happy with the results.

I'll definitely be coming back here again.

상황 1: 예약하기 (Making an appointment)

고객: Hi, I'd like to make an appointment for a haircut, please.

미용사: Of course! When would you like to come in?

고객: I'm free on Friday afternoon. Do you have any available slots then?

미용사: Yes, we have a 3:00 PM and a 4:30 PM slot open. Which one works better for you?

고객: Let's go with 3:00 PM, please.

​

상황 2: 스타일 상담 (Style consultation)

미용사: What type of hairstyle are you thinking of getting today?

고객: I'm not sure, I want something different but not too drastic. Any suggestions?

미용사: How about adding some layers and getting a side-swept fringe? It will complement your face shape nicely.

고객: That sounds great! Let's go for it.

​

상황 3: 헤어 컬러 (Hair color)

미용사: Are you interested in getting a new hair color?

고객: Yes, I want to dye my hair a shade lighter than my current color.

미용사: Sure, we can do that. Light brown or honey blonde might suit you well. What do you think?

고객: Honey blonde sounds nice. Let's do it!

​

상황 4: 잘라주기 (Getting a haircut)

미용사: How much would you like to cut off today?

고객: Please just trim the ends, and don't take off too much length.

미용사: Not a problem. We'll keep the length and give it a fresh look with a trim.

​

상황 5: 스타일링 (Styling)

미용사: How would you like your hair styled today?

고객: Can you style it with loose curls, please?

미용사: Of course! Loose curls will look great on you.

​

상황 6: 마무리 (Finishing touches)

미용사: We're all done. How do you like it?

고객: I love it! The color and the style are perfect. Thank you so much!

미용사: You're welcome! I'm glad you're happy with the result.

​

상황 7: 재방문 의사 표현 (Expressing satisfaction and future visits)

고객: I had a great experience today. I'll definitely be coming back here again.

미용사: Thank you! We look forward to seeing you again. Have a wonderful day!
