겨울철 감기조심,건강조심 덕담 영어회화 표현 모음집

​

stay warm and healthy (따뜻하고 건강하게 지내세요):

As the weather gets colder, remember to stay warm and healthy.

추운 날씨에 건강을 유지하라는 의미로 사용됩니다.

​

Keep yourself bundled up (추위에 대비해서 따뜻하게 입으세요):

Keep yourself bundled up to avoid catching a cold.

추운 날씨에 감기를 조심하라는 의미로, 따뜻한 옷을 입으라는 조언을 포함합니다.

​

Take care of yourself (몸조심하세요):

Take care of yourself and don’t get sick this winter.

겨울철 건강 관리의 중요성을 강조합니다.

​

Don’t catch a cold (감기 걸리지 마세요):

With the cold weather, make sure you don’t catch a cold.

감기 걸리지 않도록 조심하라는 당부의 의미를 담고 있습니다.

​

Stay healthy this winter (겨울에 건강하세요):

Stay healthy this winter and enjoy the holidays!

겨울철 건강을 유지하며 시즌을 즐기라는 뜻입니다.

​

Dress warmly (따뜻하게 입으세요):

Dress warmly to avoid the winter chill.

추위로부터 보호하기 위해 따뜻하게 옷을 입으라고 권유합니다.

​

Avoid getting sick (병 걸리지 않게 조심하세요):

Avoid getting sick during the cold season by taking good care of yourself.

추운 계절 동안 건강 관리를 잘 해서 병에 걸리지 않도록 조심하라는 조언입니다.

​

Keep your immune system strong (면역 체계를 강하게 유지하세요):

Keep your immune system strong to fight off the winter colds.

겨울철 감기와 싸우기 위해 면역 체계를 강화하라는 조언입니다.

​

​

대화 1: 친구 간의 대화

아나 (Ana): Hey Mike, it's getting really cold outside these days.

마이크 (Mike): Yeah, I noticed. The winter is really setting in.

​

아나: Make sure you dress warmly. You don't want to catch a cold.

마이크: Thanks, Ana. I’ll definitely bundle up. Stay warm and healthy too!

​

대화 2: 직장 동료 간의 대화

존 (John): I heard you were feeling a bit under the weather yesterday, Sarah.

사라 (Sarah): Yeah, I think the cold weather is getting to me.

​

존: Remember to keep your immune system strong. Drink plenty of fluids and get enough rest.

사라: Thanks for the advice, John. I'll try to take better care of myself.

​

대화 3: 가족 간의 대화

엄마 (Mom): It’s starting to snow a lot. Make sure you wear your scarf and hat when you go out, Emily.

에밀리 (Emily): I will, Mom. Don’t worry.

​

엄마: And don’t forget to eat fruits and vegetables to keep your immune system strong.

에밀리: I know, Mom. I’ll stay healthy this winter.

​

대화 4: 학교에서 선생님과 학생 간의 대화

선생님 (Teacher): Class, as winter is here, I want to remind you all to take care of your health.

학생 (Student): What should we do, Teacher?

​

선생님: Dress warmly, avoid getting sick, and if you feel unwell, make sure to rest at home.

학생: Thank you, Teacher. We’ll remember to stay warm and healthy.
