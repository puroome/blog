Inviting for a Party


Cher: Could you come to our party on Sunday? We’re having a baby shower.


Amy: Oh that’s nice. What should I wear? Should I bring some food?


Cher: Casual is fine. No, it’s okay. I’m doing the food. Welcoming Guests


Cher: Welcome to our home. Thank you for coming.


Amy: Thank you. Good to see you again. I have a present for you.


Cher: Thank you. Eating Together


Cher: Did you enjoy your meals?


Lyn: Yes it was very delicious.
