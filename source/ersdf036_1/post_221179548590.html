Setting Up a Date 소개팅 영어회화 및 유용한 표현






Liam: Hi Beatrice. I’ve been wanting to ask you this but… are you with someone?


Beatrice: Uhm. No.


﻿Liam: Good. Hmm… Can I ask you out then?


Beatrice: Sure. I’m free this Saturday.


Liam: Can I pick you up at 6:00?


Beatrice: Yes. I know a nice restaurant. Let’s go there.


Liam: It’s a date then.
