관공서 영어회화 및 유용한표현







1. Asking about Agencies/Department

Molly: Do you know which government agency deals with overseas nationals and

foreign relations?

Eun: Yes. You have to go to the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Molly: Thank you.


2. Processing Documents

Molly: Hello. I’d like to apply for a change of visa status.

Ha-Jun: please fill out this form first. Did you bring and I.D.?

Molly: Yes. I also brought all my requirements. 





관공서 유용한 영어회화 표현



The following are sample phrases that are useful when going to

government agencies. Think of agencies/departments where those

phrases can be used.

• Hi. Do you know where I could

apply for a passport?

• Do you know who’s in charge of…?

• Where can I get a…?

• Is the office open on Saturdays?

• What time do you open/close?

• What’s the cut-off time?

• What government agency could

help me in looking for employment?

• Do you have a telephone directory

of all government agencies?

• What documents should I fill out?

• How much is the governmental fee

for…?

• What government agency handles

housing and urban development?

• Which government agency should I

go to for assistance in

environmental protection?

• How can I process…?

• What are the requirements in

getting…?

• Can I see your identification?
