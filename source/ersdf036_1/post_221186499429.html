Doing Business Deals 사업거래하기 영어회화 및 유용한 표현



 




Mr. Thompson: If I approve of this deal, what benefits will my company get?


Mr. Morgan: The products we’ll produce are cheaper than our competitors. 

This means we get more customers, 

more profit and both our brand names will be more well-known in the industry.


Mr. Thompson: Ah I see. And what are the terms of payment?


Mr. Morgan: It’s all written in the contract.


Mr. Thompson: Hmm… I’ll have it checked later.


Mr. Morgan: So what do you think of this plan?


Mr. Thompson: I’m looking into it. I’m still considering some factors.


Mr. Morgan: Alright. Please notify me as soon as you have decided. 

Thank you for your interest and time. 

I really want to make this deal with you.


Mr. Thompson: Thank you too. I’ll have you notified.




Doing Business Deals 사업거래하기 유용한 표현



Here are some business-related phrases you can use in doing business

deals. Can you add more examples on the list?


• What is your price?


• What are the terms of payment?


• Could you give me your business card?


• Here is the contract.


• Please sign here.


• We can negotiate the price.


• We provide discounts for bulk orders.


• Can I see some samples?


• Can you send me the catalog?


• It’s a lot cheaper than our competitors.

• We’ve developed new products.


• How much do we need for the production?


• I can’t understand why we can’t work this out.


• I think I need to think things thoroughly first.


• Please reconsider your decision. I think this is a really good deal.


• We could invest on commercials.


• Let’s put out a newspaper ad.


• Thank you for doing business with us. Let’s work hard together.
