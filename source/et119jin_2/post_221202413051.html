​

시험에 나오는 부분은

​

by far + the 최상급 + 명사

= the very 최상급 + 명사

​

예를 들면,

​

틀린 부분을 고치세요.

​

She is very the most efficient worker in the office.

→ She is by far the most efficient worker in the office.
