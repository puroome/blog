■ mindfulness 

   정념, 마음챙김 

﻿ 

a psychological concept  which is the focusing of attention and awareness, based on the concept of mindfulness in Buddhist meditation. It has been popularised in the West by Jon Kabat-Zinn. Despite its roots in Buddhism, mindfulness is often taught independently of religion.

심리학적 개념으로서 주의력과 인지력을 집중하는 것을 말한다. 그 기본은 불교 명상의 정념에 바탕을 두고 있다. 존 카뱉 진에 의해 서양에 확산되었다. 불교에 뿌리를 두고 있지만 보통 종교와는 독립하여 가르쳐지고 있다.  

﻿ 

The first component of mindfulness involves the self-regulation of attention so that it is maintained on immediate experience, thereby allowing for increased recognition of mental events in the present moment. The second component involves adopting a particular orientation toward one’s experiences in the present moment, an orientation that is characterized by curiosity, openness, and acceptance. 

정념의 첫 번째 구성요소는 주의 집중력을 스스로 통제하는 것으로, 그것이 지금 당장의 경험을 향하도록 하여, 현재의 정신적 사고에 대한 인지를 증가시키도록 한다. 두 번째 구성요소는 현재 순간의 자신의 경험을 향해 특별히 방향을 설정하고, 탐구심과, 열린 마음, 그리고 수용하는 태도를 갖도록 한다. 

   

 □ The practice of mindfulness can bring many benefits to your emotional and physical health, as well as to the relationships in your life. Mindfulness is an amazing tool for stress management and overall wellness because it can be used at virtually any time and can quickly bring lasting results. The six mindfulness exercises are simple and convenient, and can lead you to a deeper experience of mindfulness in your daily life : Meditation ; Deep Breathing ; Listening to Music ; Cleaing House ; Observing You Thoughts ; Create Your Own.
