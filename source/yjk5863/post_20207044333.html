■ boilermaker

   : 폭탄주(위스키 한잔에 맥주 한 잔을 연달아 마시기); 보일러 제작자(회사)

   : A boilermaker is a trained craftsman who produces steel fabrications from plates and sections. Boilermaker may also refer to beer cocktail, a drink.
