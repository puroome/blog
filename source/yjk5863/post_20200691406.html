■ swag[수액] 

 
1. 자신만의 외모, 스타일, 개성을 표현하는 방식 혹은 그것을 갖춘 특히 젊은이
apperance ,style ,or the way he or she presents them selves.
a person who dresses fresh or clean, dresses like they have money, usually a young person 
○ He's got a killa swag. 그는 죽이게 간지난다.
 
2. swag은 자신을 간지난다고 생각하는 사람들이 특히 십대들이 남용하는 속어
A stupid saying that's overused. People 90 per cent are dumb teenagers, 10 per cent are little ass kids trying to be cool, they use it for EVERYTHING and also as their facebook name thinking that shit's cute: 'I just opened a cabinet, SWAG.' 'I just fell down,SWAG.'
 
3. 원래는 스코틀랜드 속어 swagger(뽐내며 건들건들 걷는 사람)에서 시작했으나  '자신만의 방식으로 행동하는 사람' 잘못 사용된 단어
Orginally from the Scottish slang word "swagger" which was a description of the way some Scots walk (in a swaying motion), the word was then misinterpreted by the English as "the way someone presents themselves".
