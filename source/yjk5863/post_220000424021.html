■ out(접두사) + 동사 (비교 동사)

    : (주로 접두어 out-으로 시작하는 동사로 than이 없이) (주어가) ...보다 ...

<예> outrun, outdo, outgrow, outvote, outwit, outsmart, outweigh, outlive

 

▶ outwit 더 수법이 앞서다, 더 영리하다 

▶ outplay 경기나 게임에서 더 잘 하다 

▶ outlast 더 오래 견디다 

​

 □ For many people it’s more fun to outwit, outplay and outlast a fellow human being.

[해설] it is more fun to (do) ...하는 것이 더 재미있다  fellow human being 동료인 인간

​

▶ outperform

     : ...보다 더나은 성과를 보이다

​

 □ Choi, 20, may be making life difficult for her parents, who remember a time when Korea was as poor as Bangladesh. But she is one reason why Korea's economy, amid global recession, is currently outperforming nearly every other country's in the world.

[해설] make life difficult for her parent 그녀의 부모의 삶을 어렵게 만들다 a time when ... ...한 시대 as poor as ...만큼 가난한 one reason why ... ...한 이유 recession 경기 후퇴 outperform ...보다 더 나은 성과를 거두다 nearly every other country 거의 모든 다른 나라 

​

▶ outstrip

    : ...을 따라가 앞서다, 뒤 처지게 하다(= overtake) ;

    ...보다 뛰어나다, 능가하다(= surpass, outdo) 

 

 □ In the new next two decades, the demand for nurses to take care of them will outstrip the number of nurses by 20%.

[해설] ∙demand : 수요 ∙outstrip : ...을 능가하다 ∙the number of ... : ...의 숫자

​

▶ outdo

    : ...보다 잘 하다 ​

​not to be out done 뒤지지 않으려고

 

  □ If we died in old age at the same rate we die between ages 10 and 15, then most of us in the U.S. would live 1,200 years. We would outdo the first Methuselah, whose years were 969.

[해설] ∙If we died in old age at the same rate we die between ages 10 and 15, : 만일 우리가 10살이나 15살 나이에 죽는 것과 같은 비율로 노년의 나이에 죽는 다면 ∙MethuseIah : (聖) 므두셀라 (노아 호수 이전의 족장으로서 969세까지 산 장수자) as old as Methuselah : 아주 나이가 많은

 

▶ outmaneuver

    : ...을 술책으로 이기다, 허를 찌르다

    : outfox, outsmart, outwit

outsmart oneself : 제 꾀에 넘어가다

be outmaneuvered : (주어가) 술책에 당하다[지다]

​

 □ After government lawyers last negotiated a settlement with Microsoft, the firm still felt free to bundle its Internet browsing software with Windows―a central issue in the current case. The DOJ does not want to be outmaneuvred again.

[해설]negotiated a settlement : 해결책을 협상해 만들에 내다  feel free to (do) : ...하는 데있어 자유를 느끼다  bundle A with B  A와 B를 번들 상품으로 묶어 팔다   the DOJ = the Department of Justice 법무부  never be outmaneuvered again 결코 소송에서 지다
