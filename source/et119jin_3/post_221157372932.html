cancel [|kӕnsl]
(동사) 취소하다

cancel is to say that something that has been arranged will not now happen

something that has been arranged(예정된 어떤것이)
will not happen(일어나지 않을 것)

예문을 볼게요.

All flights have been cancelled because of bad weather.
날씨가 나빠 모든 항공기 운항이 취소되었다.
I'm afraid I have to cancel our Thursday appointment.
죄송하지만 목요일 약속을 취소해야겠는데요.

cancer [|kӕnsə(r)]
(명사) 암

cancer is a serious illness caused by a group of cells in the body increasing in an uncontrolled way.

예문을 볼게요.

Most skin cancers are completely curable.
피부암은 대부분이 완전 치유가 가능하다.
The U.S. death rate from cancer increased last year.
작년 미국의 암 사망률이 높아졌다.
I realized for the first time how dreadful cancer is.
암의 무서움을 비로소 알았다.
