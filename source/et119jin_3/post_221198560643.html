conservation [|kɑ:nsər|veɪʃn]
(명사) 보호, 보존, 관리

conservation is the management of land and water in ways that prevent it from being damaged or destroyed

management of land and water(땅과 물 관리) /
prevent it from being damaged or destroyed(피해를 입거나 파괴되는 것으로부터 막는)

결국 saving and protecting
의미네요.

예문으로 의미를 익혀보세요.

a nature conservation movement
자연보호 운동

conservation of mass
질량 보존(의 법칙)

energy conservation
에너지 관리

conversation [|kɑ:nvər|seɪʃn]
(명사) 대화

conversation is a talk between two or more people, usually a private and informal one

a talk(대화) /
between two or more people(둘 혹은 더 많은 사람들과의)

예문으로 의미를 익혀보세요.

I had a long conversation with her the other day.
