corporate
(형용사) 회사의, 법인의, 공동의

corporate is relating to large companies, or to a particular large company

relateing to compaines(회사와 관련)

예문으로 의미를 익혀보세요.

corporate strategy
기업 전략

The BBC is a corporate body.

corporate responsibility

incorporate
(동사) 포함하다, 설립하다

1) incorporate is to add or include something as a part of something else
2) incorporate is to form a corporation

1) add or include(더하거나 포함시키다)
2) form a corporation(회사를 설립하다)
