career [kə|rɪr]
(명사) 직업, 사회생활

career is a job or series of related jobs that you do, especially a profession that you spend a lot of your working life in

예문을 볼게요.

What made you decide on a career as a vet?
당신이 수의사를 직업으로 삼을 결심을 하게 된 계기는 무엇이었습니까?
She started her career as an English teacher.
그녀는 영어 교사로 사회생활을 시작했다.

carrier [|kӕriə(r)]
(명사) 운반하는 사람, 매개체, 회사

1) a company that moves goods or people from one place to another
2) someone who can pass a genetic disease to their children without suffering from it themselves
3) a metal frame attached to a bicycle and used for carrying things
4) a vehicle or ship used for moving goods or people, especially soldiers

예문을 볼게요.

A mail carrier is someone who delivers mail to people.
우편 배달부는 사람들에게 편지를 배달하는 사람이다. 
 The tiger mosquito is a carrier of the dengue fever virus.
외줄모기는 뎅기열의 매개체이다.
How about talking with another insurance carrier?
다른 보험 회사와 의논해 보는 게 어떨까요?
