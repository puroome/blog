﻿설상가상으로,

 

엎친 데 덮친 격으로,

 

 

 

 

to add insult to injury,

 

to make matters worse,

 

to make things worse,

 

what is worse,

 

When it rains, it pours.

 

 

 

 

- I had a fight with my wife this morning, and to add insult toinjury I got a ticket on my way to work.

   오늘 아침 마누라와 싸웠는데 설상가상으로 출근길에 교통딱지까지 받았어.

 

- To make matters worse, Cindy divorced her husband.

   엎친 데 덮친 격으로 신디는 남편과 이혼했다.

 

- What is worse, it started to rain.

   설상가상으로 비까지 내리기 시작했다.
