A : Mom, I'm getting really tired.  Can I take a break?

     엄마, 정말로 피곤해서 그러는데요, 잠깐 쉬다 하면 안될까요?

 

B : Keep it up just a little longer and then you can rest.

     조금만 더 열심히 하고 쉬거라.

 

 

A : I sold 10 cars last month.  That's my best month this year.

 B : That's great Cindy.  Keep up the good work.
