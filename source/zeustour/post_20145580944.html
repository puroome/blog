People often believe that a person is like his or her friends. 

Your reputation can be either harmed or enhanced by association.

 

사람은 보통 자기 친구와 비슷하다는 말이 있습니다. 

당신의 명성은 당신이 어울리는 사람들에 의해서 향상될 수도 있고 손상을 입을 수도 있습니다.

 

 

Father : SOn, where have you been last night?

Son : I was playing with friends at the playground.

Father : High school students hanging out at the playground at night?

           That doesn't sound right. You guys might be smoking?

Son : No, dad. We were just talking.

Father : Son, a man is known by the company he keeps.

            I would like you to have more academic friends.
