What’s your love story? You know, that story that has a place in your mind. Who is this person in your love story, and why does it matter? Well, there are soul mates and life partners, and they are 2 completely different things.

Some people spend a lifetime searching for their soul mate without thinking about what “soul mate” even means. In fact, our soul mates are people who are present in our lives to challenge, awaken, or stir up different parts of us in order for us to grow. These people are often fleeting because once the lesson is learned, the relationship is usually over. And as the term “life partners” denotes, these people are usually in it for the long haul.



SUMMARY:
- Neither soul mates nor life partners implies or excludes romantic involvement. They can be friends or lovers or people who enter your life briefly when you need them and then quickly fade out.

​
- When people are asking or searching for their soul mate, they are actually asking and searching for a way to grow and level-up their consciousness. If we don’t understand this, there can be a huge disconnect, and we can sometimes miss out on this energy exchange or growth.

​
- As far as soul mates go, you need to start by listening to your heart. Through this, you will discover who you are. Once you realize your true nature and are open to your life’s lessons, your vibrations will attract those of your soul mates.

​
- A life partner is someone you think of when you are thinking about true companionship or friendship. That deep soul connection might not be there as it would with a soul mate, but there are other important feelings that are the foundation of this type of relationship.

​
- Your initial meeting with your life partner and soul mate might also differ. You won’t have to prepare yourself energetically to meet your life partner like you would with your soul mate, but you would have to physically put yourself out there.

​
- The most basic of the human psychological needs are security and love. These things help us thrive as human beings, and they need to be satisfied for us to attain higher level needs like being creative, feeling successful, or reaching our full potential.
