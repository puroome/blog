"pleasant"와 "pleasing"은 의미가 다소 혼동스러울 수 있어요. 아래의 설명을 따르면 "pleasant" 상쾌한 날씨나 호감가는 인상처럼 좋은 느낌을 주는 특성을 의미하는 단어예요. 이와 달리 "pleasing"은 기대하는 바나 원하는 바를 충족시켜서 기쁨을 주는 것을 의미해요.

​

I gave this quite a bit of thought and have come to this conclusion: pleasant directly refers to the noun having qualities which give pleasure(e.g.: That was a pleasant afternoon. - The afternoon was pleasant.) whereas pleasing refers to the act of giving pleasure(e.g.: That was a pleasing book. - The book gave the reader pleasure.)
