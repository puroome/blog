prognosis

​

"prognosis(예후, 예측)"는 주로 의학적인 용어로 사용되지만 "예측"이라는 의미로도 사용될 수 있어요. Cambridge 영영사전에서는 다음과 같이 설명하고 있어요.

​

예후: a doctor's judgment of the likely or expected development of a disease or of the chances of getting better.

The prognosis after the operation was for a full recovery.

​

예측: a statement of what is judged likely to happen in the future, especially in connection with a particular situation.

I was reading a gloomy economic prognosis in the paper this morning.
