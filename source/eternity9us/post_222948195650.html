​

Difference between Stimulant and Stimulus - Writing Tips

Stimulant refers to drugs or other things that increase activity. Caffeine, a mild stimulant, acts on the central nervous system. Stimulus refers to an incentive that initiates activity. Music alone can provide a stimulus to a person’s imagination.
