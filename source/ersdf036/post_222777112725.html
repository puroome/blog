감각을 나타내는 동사 ▷ look, seem, smell, sound, taste 등


Every fish that escapes [appears] greater than it is.

This paper [feels] smooth.

Farming [looks] mighty easy.

The stars [seem] very permanent.  

The rose [smells] sweet.

The story [sounds] strange.

A good medicine [tastes] bitter.


 

지각을 나타내는 동사 ▷ see, watch, look at, hear, listen to, feel, notice 등

I watched a cat crossing the street.

I heard someone talk on the phone.

I feel my heart beat.

I saw him kicking the soccer ball.

She watched the kids play in the yard.

I didn't notice him leave.

I listened carefully the children talk each other.

They observed him cook dinner.

She could smell the eggs go bad.

The buzzer sounded a special class end.

The polieman looked the thief run away.

=>감각동사 


see 주어의 의지로 보는 것으로 

보다로 해석됩니다

=> 지각동사 



감각을 나타내는 동사는 2형식(S + V + C)에 쓰이고,

지각을 나타내는 동사는 5형식(S + V + O + O.C)에 쓰여요.


감각을 나타낼 때는 C 자리에 명사나 형용사가 오고,

지각을 나타낼 때는 C(OC) 자리에 동사원형, 현재분사, 과거분사가 와요.
