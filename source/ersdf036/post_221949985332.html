Give it to whoever wants it. (= anyone who : wants의 주어) 

Give it to whomever you like. (= anyone whom : like의 목적어) 


whoever, whomever 고르는 문제는 

뒤의 문장 속에서 주어로 쓰이면 whoever, 

목적어로 쓰이면 whomever 입니다. 


관계사에 -ever를 붙인 것을 복합 관계사라고 합니다.

복합관계대명사와

복합관계부사가 있어요.






1) 복합관계대명사


Whoever wants it may take it. 

= Anyone who wants it may take it.

누구든지 그것을 원하는 사람이 가져도 좋다.



2) 복합관계부사


Whenever I am in trouble, I consult him. 

= At any time (that) I am in trouble, ~.

나는 곤란에 빠질 때마다 그와 상의한다.




1. Black pepper was imported from South India and nearby islands.

2. Black pepper was an expensive spice.

겹치는 부분이 black pepper인 상황에서, 


주절을 무엇으로 할지(즉, 어떤 문장을 관계사절로 할지)를 결정해야 합니다



즉,  마치 공식처럼 적용하면 ~ 아래 2가지가 가능할 수있습니다.

- Black pepper that was an expensive spice was imported from South India and nearby islands.

- Black pepper that was imported from South India and nearby islands was an expensive spice.


하지만, 두번째가 상대적으로 적절해 보입니다. 

=> Black pepper ​that was imported from South India and nearby islands ​was an expensive spice.



왜냐면, 첫번째 문장은 아래처럼 (관계사절이 아닌) 형용사를 사용해서 표현이 가능하기 때문이기도 합니다

- Expensive black pepper was imported from South India and nearby islands.

- An expensive spice of black pepper was imported from South India and nearby islands.

=> 양보와 부정(정해지지 않음)
