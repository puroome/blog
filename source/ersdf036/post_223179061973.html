remind 와 비슷한 단어

​

Prompt: 자극하다, 촉발하다.

Her question prompted him to think deeply about his decision.

​

Alert: 경고하다, 주의를 주다.

The alarm system will alert us if there's any unusual activity.

​

Notify: 통보하다, 알리다.

They will notify you by email once your package is shipped.

​

Recollect: 기억하다, 상기시키다.

He tried to recollect the events of that fateful day.

​

Evoke: 불러일으키다, 상기시키다.

The old photograph evoked memories of their adventures.

​

Jog Memory: 기억을 자극하다, 상기시키다.

The familiar smell of cookies jogged her memory of baking with her grandmother.

​

Prompting: 자극, 촉발.

The teacher's prompting led the students to explore the topic further.

​

Signal: 신호를 보내다, 알리다.

The sudden change in weather signaled the approaching storm.

​

Spark: 일으키다, 자극하다.

Their conversation sparked an interesting debate among the group.

​

Prod: 충동적으로 자극하다.

The new evidence prodded the investigators to reopen the case.

​

Stimulate: 자극하다, 격려하다.

The workshop aimed to stimulate creativity among participants.

​

Invoke: 불러일으키다, 상기시키다.

The song's lyrics invoke a sense of nostalgia for many listeners.

​

Stir up: 자극하다, 불러일으키다.

The speech stirred up emotions and motivated the audience.

​

Prod someone's memory: 누군가의 기억을 자극하다.

The old photographs prodded her memory of their childhood adventures.

remind 사용한 다양한 표현

​

Remind me of: ~를 저에게 상기시키다.

This song reminds me of my childhood.

​

Remind someone to do something: 누군가에게 어떤 일을 하라고 상기시키다.

Can you remind me to call the doctor tomorrow?

​

Remind someone of something: 누군가에게 어떤 것을 상기시키다.

The old house reminded him of his grandparents' home.

​

Remindful: 주의를 기울이는, 상기시키는.

The painting is remindful of a beautiful sunset.

​

Constantly remind: 지속적으로 상기시키다.

She constantly reminds herself to stay positive.

​

Remind someone about something: 누군가에게 어떤 것에 대해 상기시키다.

I need to remind John about the meeting this afternoon.

​

Remind someone of their manners: 누군가에게 예의를 상기시키다.

His mother always reminds him of his manners at the dinner table.

​

Remind someone of the consequences: 누군가에게 결과를 상기시키다.

The teacher reminded the students of the consequences of

​

Remind someone of their responsibilities: 누군가에게 책임을 상기시키다.

The supervisor reminded the employees of their responsibilities.

​

Remindful of the past: 과거를 상기시키는.

The ruins were remindful of the once-thriving civilization.

​

Remind yourself: 자신에게 상기시키다.

Whenever she feels stressed, she reminds herself to take deep breaths.

​

Remind someone gently: 누군가에게 부드럽게 상기시키다.

He gently reminded her to complete the assignment.

​

Remind someone of an appointment: 누군가에게 약속을 상기시키다.

Can you remind me of my dentist appointment next week?

​

Remind someone of the deadline: 누군가에게 마감일을 상기시키다.

The boss reminded the team of the upcoming project deadline.

​

Remind someone of the rules: 누군가에게 규칙을 상기시키다.

The coach reminded the players of the team's code of conduct.

​

Remindful of the importance: 중요성을 상기시키는.

The tragic event was remindful of the importance of safety precautions.

​

Remind someone not to forget: 누군가에게 잊지 말라고 상기시키다.

She reminded her friend not to forget to bring the tickets.

​

Frequently remind: 자주 상기시키다.

It's important to frequently remind yourself of your goals.
