not는 명사수식이 가능한 부사이므로 not too much time은 맞는 표현입니다.


명사/대명사 수식 부사; even, just, not, often, only, rather, quiet, arguably, definitely, exactly, especially. essentially, fundamentally, largely, mostly, particularly, partly, scarcely, simply, specifically, understandably 


​{Not} a sound came from the room.


He has {not} a bike.


not 은 부사로 부정하고자 하는 말(단어, 구, 절 )앞에 놓입니다.(물론, 명사 앞에도 가능 하고요)

not +[명사, 부사, 준동사, 구, 절 ]



Not everybody agrees.

Not a man spoke to him.

He spoke not a word.


no +명사 = not ~any/a(an) +명사 (하나의 ~도없는 )의 강조의미입니다.


No teachers went on strike.

=There were not any teachers on strikes.

The students went on strike, but not the teachers ( no the teachers )
