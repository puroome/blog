공항에서 자주쓰는 영어회화표현

입국 관련 표현:

"I have a connecting flight to [도착지]." (저는 [도착지]로의 연결 항공편이 있습니다.)

"Could you please stamp my passport?" (여권을 찍어주시겠어요?)

"Where can I pick up my baggage?" (수하물을 어디에서 찾을 수 있나요?)

"Is there a customs declaration form?" (세관 신고서가 있나요?)

​

출국 관련 표현:

"I'd like to check in for my flight to [출발지]." ([출발지]행 비행기 체크인을 하려고 합니다.)

"What's the gate number for my flight?" (제 비행기의 탑승구 번호가 무엇인가요?)

"How much is the excess baggage fee?" (초과 수하물 요금은 얼마인가요?)

"Is my flight delayed?" (제 비행기가 지연되었나요?)

​

통과 절차 관련 표현:

"Do I need to take off my shoes?" (신발을 벗어야 하나요?)

"Is there a separate line for families?" (가족을 위한 별도의 줄이 있나요?)

"Can I bring a bottle of water on the plane?" (비행기 안에 물 병을 가져갈 수 있나요?)

"Do I need to show my ID?" (신분증을 제시해야 하나요?)

​

도움 요청 표현:

"Could you please help me find my gate?" (제게 탑승구를 찾는 데 도움을 주시겠어요?)

"Can you assist me with my luggage?" (수하물을 도와주실 수 있나요?)

"Is there a lost and found desk here?" (여기서 분실물 센터가 있나요?)

"Could you tell me where the nearest restroom is?" (가장 가까운 화장실이 어디인지 알려주시겠어요?)

​

공항에서 있을법한 다이얼로그

체크인

A: Good morning. I'd like to check in for my flight to London, please.

B: Good morning. May I see your passport and ticket, please?

A: Sure, here they are.

B: Thank you. Here's your boarding pass. Your gate number is 12. Enjoy your flight!

A: Thank you.

​

수하물 문의

A: Excuse me, where can I pick up my baggage?

B: Baggage claim is located on the lower level. Follow the signs, and you'll find it easily.

A: Great, thank you!

​

비행기 지연

A: Is my flight to Paris delayed? I haven't seen any updates on the departure board.

B: I apologize for the inconvenience. Yes, there's been a slight delay due to bad weather. We expect the departure time to be pushed back by 30 minutes.

A: Alright, thank you for letting me know.

​

세관 신고

A: Excuse me, is there a customs declaration form that I need to fill out?

B: Yes, here it is. Please complete it and declare any items that exceed the duty-free allowance.

A: Thank you for your help.

​

비행기 탑승

A: Could you please help me find my gate? I seem to be a bit lost.

B: Of course. What's your destination?

A: I'm flying to Sydney.

B: Alright, your gate is D7. Follow this corridor, and you'll reach it on your right.

A: Thank you so much for your assistance.
