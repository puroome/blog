방향위치전치사(into,over,on,up,down,among,though,across) 사용표현 뽀개기


 

1.to ~로,으로 (도착점) , from ~에서 (시작점)
I am going to school.


2.into ~로, 안으로 , (... 방향)으로 , ~에 대고 [~을 향해 ]
He went into our room.

3. out of ~ 밖에
I took the old batteries out of the radio.


4.on ~위에,(무엇의 표면위에),무엇에 기대거나 받쳐져 있음
Don't put your feet on the table.


5. off, 내리다,떨어지다.
Please take your feet off the table.


6.up ~위에(위쪽으로 ,방향) , down ~아래로 (아래쪽으로,방향)
We walked up the hill to the house.
be careful! Don't fall down the stairs.



7.over ~넘어(위로 넘어 완전히 덮는 ,거리 공간를 넘는 )
The plane flew over the mountains.


6.though ~지나서(통과해서)
A bird flew into the room through a window.


8.along ~따라서
Let's go for a walk along the river.


9,. across 
the dog swarm across the river.


10.past ~지나서(건물따위를 지나치는)
they walked past me without speaking.
